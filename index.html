Overview

Wentworth Reunion is an interactive artwork that uses the device camera to display augmented reality scenes. The app is designed for museums and public installations.

Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal data. We do not create user accounts. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Permissions and how they are used

  • Camera: Required to render AR content and to detect target images. Live camera input stays on the device and is not transmitted or stored by the developer.
  • Microphone: Not used unless you enable a feature that needs audio in a future update. If that ever changes the permission request will be clear and this policy will be updated.
  • Photos/Files: Not used by this version of the app.

Third-party services

The app uses Apple AR frameworks (ARKit/RealityKit) provided by iOS. No third-party analytics or advertising services are integrated.

Children

The app can be used by families and young people but does not collect any personal information from users.

Data security

No personal data is collected by the developer. Camera imagery is processed in real time on your device.

Your choices

You can disable camera permission in iOS Settings at any time. The app will not function without camera access.
